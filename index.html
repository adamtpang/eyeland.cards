How it plays

Island-hopping meets card duels.

The feeling is walking into a strange new town, finding a creature, and solving trouble with the deck in your hand.

Open world · like Pokémon

Explore the Eyeland

Wander a living archipelago of biomes, hidden coves, and wild encounters. Every island is a small mystery past the horizon.

Deckbuilding · like Hearthstone

Build your spellbook

Collect spell-cards and creature companions across the world, then craft the deck that fits your style.

Card duels · like Wizard101

Win the duel

Combat is a hand of cards. Bank your pips, chain spells, summon allies, and read your opponent.

The deck

Your spellbook is a deck.
